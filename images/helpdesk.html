Our Radicle Approach

Radicle Health acquires mission critical human services software companies.

We believe technology is at the root of success in the human services sector, but that no single system can meet the needs of every agency. We provide our companies the autonomy to focus on their unique customers, but with a culture of collaboration to do more together.
